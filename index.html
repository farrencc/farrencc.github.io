Research

  • Electoral Redistricting Project - 2023

    • Instiutional trust is sorely lacking in the modern western world. Too often the strategy of going over the people is employed - leading to ever compounding distrust. Ultimately this leads to a sorely disfunctioning society. So then we ask, what can we do to fix the situation? Towards finding an answer, the TPSA ran a hackathon in May of 2023 alongside TCD's School of Maths. Following on from the results of this, I developed a genetic algorithm to redistrict electoral constituencies in Ireland.   • Oxford University Internship - Summer 2024

    • I was lucky enough to be given the opportunity to participate in the Rudolf Peierls Centre for Theoretical Physics UROP at Oxford University. I got to research Renormalon effects on a specific class of observables found in \( e^- e^+ \) annihilation events under Prof. Gavin P. Salam FRS, Prof. Fabrizio Caola, Jack Oliver Helliwell, and Silvia Zanoli. These observables, known as event shape observables yield information into the geometry of the event taking place. I used the software to simulate electron positron annihilation to quark anti-quark pair events as well as corrections involving soft gluons and gluers to analyse the change in those event shapes.
